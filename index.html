Why are we doing this?

One fine day, in a world filled with curious minds, a group of friends gathered to explore the infinite possibilities of Artificial Intelligence. Among them was an AI practitioner, the host of the gathering. As they delved into spirited debates, they realized that the power of AI was undeniable - there was no escaping it. The question was whether to let AI take control or to take control of AI.

They knew they needed to embrace the future and harness the potential of AI for their benefit. Eager to embark on this journey, they set out to find comprehensive resources to master AI. However, frustration loomed large as they encountered complicated materials, and courses with hefty prices(like 50k n above) that left them bewildered. It seemed that a lack of accessible, user-friendly resources was a common issue for many AI enthusiasts. Inspired by their frustration and the success of their AI practitioner friend's weekend teaching sessions, the group decided to bring their offline learning experience to the online world for fraction price of courses. Thus, the doPrompt Club was born!

What is Prompting in Ai world/Prompt Engineering?

Technically speaking, Prompting typically refers to providing an input or instructions to an AI model like Chatgpt, Midjourney, to generate a specific response or perform a particular task.
‍
Normally speaking, Prompting is just a way to communicate with AI models like coding to apps.

Hence, entering into the new world of communication.
